Me

Hi, I’m Amit Agni. I am a data specialist at Virgin Australia. My day job involves extracting/analysing airline data to create data-driven products for sales, e-commerce and finance departments. I have been in the tech industry for over 20 years and worked across India, UAE, USA and Australia.

Since 2016, I have focused my career on data analytics and data science. I mostly use R for my work and intend to use Python in the near future.

I am an eternal learner so love to update myself with latest happenings in the data world.

Data Learnings

This is my personal blog in which I write about everything in the data domain. In the blog, you will find

  • the notes that I have taken while studying online courses and while reading books
  • the exploratory analysis and predictive models created as part of data competitions like Kaggle
  • documentation of my personal projects

I hope the blog helps you in your own data journey. If you’d like to connect/collaborate with me, you can add me on Linkedin or email me. I host my projects and repositories on github
